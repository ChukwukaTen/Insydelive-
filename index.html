Terms of Service

Draft — last updated July 2026. This has not been reviewed by a lawyer yet; treat it as a starting point, not a finished legal document, before launch.

1. Who can use InsydeLive

You must be at least 18 years old to create an account. InsydeLive is a social app for nightlife venues — by using it you confirm you meet the minimum legal age for the country you're in, including for any venue you check into.

2. Your account

You're responsible for the activity on your account and for keeping your login credentials secure. You can request deletion of your account at any time from Profile → Privacy & data.

3. What InsydeLive is — and isn't

InsydeLive helps you see who's checked into a venue and connect with them — pass requests, gifts, and follows. InsydeLive does not sell, serve, or deliver alcohol or any other substance, is not a ticketing or venue-booking service, and is not responsible for venue admission, safety, or conduct once you're there. Any gift sent through the app (e.g. "buy someone a drink") is a social gesture recorded in-app — it is not a real payment or purchase unless a specific venue has separately integrated payment, which is not part of this build.

4. Conduct

Don't harass, threaten, or impersonate anyone; don't use the app to facilitate anything illegal; don't attempt to access another user's account or scrape the platform. We can suspend or remove accounts that violate this.

5. Safety

The optional emergency contact info you can add in Profile → Safety is for your own reference and is only shared with anyone else if you choose to press the SOS button. SOS notifies people currently at the same venue as you and opens a pre-written text to your emergency contact on your own phone — it is a best-effort tool, not a monitoring service, and does not replace calling emergency services directly if you're in immediate danger. We cannot guarantee anyone will see or respond to an SOS alert in time.

6. Liability

InsydeLive is provided "as is." To the extent permitted by law, we're not liable for what happens at a venue, between users, or as a result of connections made through the app. This section in particular needs a qualified lawyer's review before launch — liability language is jurisdiction-specific and what's written here is not a substitute for that review.

7. Changes

We may update these terms as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Privacy Policy

Draft — last updated July 2026. This describes what the current build actually collects and does; have it reviewed by someone qualified in UK/EU GDPR and Nigerian NDPA before launch, since it will need to be a legally binding document, not just an accurate one.

What we collect

Account info you provide directly: name, username, email, country, phone number (optional). Profile details you choose to add: bio, vibe, interests, photo. Activity you generate: venue check-ins, pass requests, gifts sent/received, who you follow. Optional safety info: an emergency contact and notes, stored separately from your public profile. Technical info: device/browser type for push notifications, and cookie consent choices.

Location — only when you press SOS

We don't track your location in the background, ever, and check-ins are something you actively do, not passive tracking. The one exception: if you press the SOS button, we capture your device's location at that single moment to include in the alert sent to people at your venue. This is a one-time snapshot tied to that specific alert, not continuous tracking, and it only happens when you deliberately trigger it. Your browser will ask permission at that moment; if you decline, the SOS still sends, just without a location attached.

What we don't collect

Outside of the SOS case above, we don't access your location, your contacts, photos beyond ones you choose to upload, or other apps on your device.

How we use it

To run the core features you'd expect: showing you who's checked into a venue, delivering pass requests and gifts, sending notifications (in-app and push, only for categories you haven't turned off in Profile → Notifications). We do not sell your data to third parties or use it to serve ads within InsydeLive.

Who we share it with

Supabase, our database and infrastructure provider, processes data on our behalf under a standard data processing arrangement. Push notifications are delivered via each browser's own push service (e.g. Google, Mozilla, Apple) as a technical requirement of how Web Push works — they receive only an encrypted message, not your personal data. We don't share your data with venues, advertisers, or anyone else without your explicit action (e.g. choosing to send a pass request, or pressing SOS). When you press SOS, your location at that moment is shared with people currently checked in or going out at the same venue as you — not your followers generally, not anyone else. We don't text your emergency contact ourselves; pressing SOS opens a pre-written message in your own phone's messaging app for you to send, which we never see or store.

Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / NDPA)

You can request a full export of your data or request deletion of your account at any time from Profile → Privacy & data. We aim to respond to these requests within 30 days, as required by GDPR. You can also withdraw cookie/analytics consent at any time from the same screen, and turn off individual notification categories from Profile → Notifications.

Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, your account and associated personal data are removed, though some records (like consent logs) may be retained briefly where we have a legal obligation to do so.

International transfers

InsydeLive is a UK-registered service used internationally, including in Nigeria and other West African countries. Your data may be processed in a different country from the one you're in; where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards for that transfer.

Children's privacy

InsydeLive is not intended for anyone under 18, and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under that age.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or to exercise your data rights directly instead of through the app: privacy@insydelive.com (replace with your real contact before launch).
